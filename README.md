# 🍫 Chocolate Sales Performance Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview
This Business Intelligence project focuses on extracting, modeling, and analyzing sales performance data for an Australian chocolate manufacturing company. The analysis covers historical revenue, profit distributions, product brand metrics, and customer segments to deliver strategic expansion insights.

## 🎯 Strategic Key Insights
* **Geographic Expansion:** Sales are heavily concentrated in Australia. The analysis identifies massive untapped potential in the United States and India (the world's most populous market with strong trade connections to Australia).
* **Product Brand Performance:** Evaluates revenue across primary brands, highlighting a need to deep-dive into underperforming product lines like *Velvet Cocoa* and *SweetPeak*.
* **Customer Segment Distribution:** Explores quantity sold and profit share across diverse customer profiles.

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation
* **Data Modeling:** Built a robust star schema relational model connecting sales facts with dimension tables.
* **DAX & Metrics:** Developed custom measures to analyze Revenue vs. Cost trends and identify top-performing employees and customer tiers.
* **Reporting:** Documented findings in an executive performance report with targeted recommendations for future market entries.
